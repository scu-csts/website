Solar Sister

Three Global Social Benefit Fellows, select junior undergraduate students at Santa Clara University, conducted research on social entrepreneurship and solar lighting technologies in Uganda with three GSBI enterprises in 2013: Solar Sister (GSBI 2011), Angaza Design (GSBI 2011), and Kiva.org (GSBI 2006). They investigated the social benefits gained by Solar Sister micro-entrepreneurs and researched the feasibility of a new solar lighting product in Uganda.
