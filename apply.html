Apply

Santa Clara University students are eligible to apply in winter quarter of their junior year.
There are three stages to the application.

Attend a GSBF info session between December 1, 2014 and January 14, 2015 at CSTS in Nobili Hall.

Register your basic information and project placement preferences starting December 1, 2014. Be sure you have reviewed the proposals for 2015.

After you register, within 48 hours we will send you an email with instructions on how to submit your required documentation (GSBF personal application essay, academic transcript, and three letters of recommendation).
Read detailed instructions on how to apply for the fellowship, including the personal application, letters of recommendation and the fellowship timeline here.
